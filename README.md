# ClearOut
ClearOut is a native Android phone cleaner that does what every other cleaner app promises but none actually deliver

What ClearOut is

ClearOut is a native Android phone cleaner that does what every other cleaner app promises but none actually deliver — honest, on-device scanning with no ads, no fake warnings, and no subscription trap. It scans your real photos, real apps, and real notifications using Android's own APIs, and shows you exactly what's wasting space or draining your attention.
The monetisation angle is the product's best feature as a business: every competitor either charges £25/year with ads still running (CCleaner), or gives everything away for free with no revenue (Files by Google). ClearOut sits in the gap — £2.99/year, no ads ever, genuinely more capable than the free tools.

The full feature set
Free for everyone:

Health score dashboard with animated ring (0–100, calculated from real scan data)
Large files scanner — crawls Downloads, DCIM, Movies, Music, Documents and flags anything over 50 MB
Unused apps detector — uses Android's UsageStatsManager to find apps not opened in 90+ days, triggers the real system uninstall dialog
Reclaimable storage estimate

Pro (£2.99/year, 7-day free trial):

AI duplicate photo grouping — scans MediaStore for burst shots taken within 10 seconds of each other, groups them, picks the best quality, lets you delete the rest
App usage heatmap — custom canvas view showing screen time per app over 7 days, colour-coded from cool to hot, with a ranked list below
Notification spam audit — NotificationListenerService counts real notification volume per app, lets you silence the worst offenders
All future Pro features included automatically
