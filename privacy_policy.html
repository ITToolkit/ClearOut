ClearOut App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version: ClearOut does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any of your personal data. Everything runs entirely on your device. We have no servers. We see nothing.

What data ClearOut accesses

To do its job, ClearOut reads information from your device. Here is exactly what it accesses and why:

  • Photos and media — to find duplicate and blurry images in your photo library. Files are read locally and never uploaded.
  • App usage statistics — to identify apps you haven't opened recently. This data stays on your device.
  • Notification access — to count how many notifications each app sends per day. Notification content is never read or stored.
  • Storage / file system — to find large files and orphaned app data folders. Files are only shown to you; nothing is sent anywhere.
  • Installed app list — to detect apps no longer installed that have left data behind.

What data ClearOut does NOT collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, location)
  • No analytics or usage tracking of any kind
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No crash reporting to external services
  • No photo content, file content, or notification content
  • No data is ever transmitted to any server

Subscriptions and payments

ClearOut Pro is purchased through Google Play. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google. ClearOut never sees or stores any payment information. Your subscription status is verified directly between the app and Google Play's billing servers.

Permissions explained

ClearOut requests the following Android permissions:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — read your photos to find duplicates
  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS — see which apps you've opened recently
  • BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE — count notifications per app (not read content)
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES — detect installed apps to find orphaned files
  • com.android.vending.BILLING — process Pro subscription via Google Play

All permissions are used solely for the features described above. No permission is used for tracking or advertising.

Children's privacy

ClearOut does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Because no data is collected at all, ClearOut is safe for use on any device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date. Given that ClearOut collects no data, material changes are unlikely.
